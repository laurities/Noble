About

The analyst behind the ledger.

I'm a Business Analyst with nearly three years of progressive experience in digital banking at HDFC Bank, where I sit between Operations, IT, Compliance and third-party vendors to turn regulatory mandates and manual bottlenecks into automated, measurable systems.

My work spans requirements engineering, SQL and Power BI reporting, and REST API integration, all inside end-to-end SDLC delivery run on Agile/Scrum. I'm most useful on projects where a process has become unwieldy — long approval chains, manual reconciliation, unclear ownership — and needs to be re-mapped, documented and rebuilt so it holds up under audit.

Outside the day-to-day delivery work, I care about writing requirements that don't need to be re-explained twice, and about leaving a process better documented than I found it.
