RBI Email ID Validation Compliance

Regulatory · Compliance

Problem

A new RBI mandate required verified email IDs across four separate core banking systems, with no existing cross-system validation process and a strict compliance deadline.

Approach

Coordinated IT, Operations and Compliance; authored detailed BRDs and FRDs to remove ambiguity from requirements before development began, and tracked delivery against the regulatory deadline.

Result

Full regulatory compliance achieved across all four systems on time, with clearer documentation reducing rework — recognised with HDFC Bank's Silver Star Award.

CASA & Fixed Deposit Lien Journey Automation

Process Automation

Problem

Lien marking and release on CASA and FD accounts was a manual process taking up to seven hours per case, with no built-in approval control.

Approach

Designed an end-to-end automated journey with Maker/Checker approval controls, replacing manual validation steps with system-enforced checks.

Result

Processing time dropped to under ten minutes per case, with recurring monthly operational savings for the bank and a fully auditable approval trail.

97%faster (7 hrs → <10 min)
₹3.5Lsaved monthly
